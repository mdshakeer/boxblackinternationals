Rebar Detailing: The concept of readymade steel, where we detail the bar bending schedule with the rebar representation drawing for better understanding and rebar arrangement in construction site. The curtailment and bar bend schedules will be extracted in to a barlist file in a specified format. For feasible fabrication in steel factories.

Estimation: Summarized reports for detailed estimate of overall project will help in tendering process, initial pre-construction process for better commercial values. Structural steel Modelling for selecting an appropriate member by load and other important considerations. And structural steel detailing is for incorporating informations in it for erection and fixing.
